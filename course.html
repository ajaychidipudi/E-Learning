Technical and Personality Developemental Courses

ENGINEERING

B.E / B.TECH | M.E / M.TECH

The creative application of scientific principles to design or develop structures, machines, apparatus, or manufacturing processes.

MEDICAL

BAMS | B.SC(MEDICINE) | BHMS | BPT

A degree in medicine will give you skills in good medical practice; in evidence-based medicine; in dealing with difficult or emergency situations and in investigating and diagnosing medical conditions.

PHARMACY

B.PHARM | M.PHARM

Pharmacy is the clinical health science that links medical science with chemistry and it is charged with the discovery, production, disposal, safe and effective use, and control of medications and drugs.

DENTAL

BDS | MDS

Dentistry courses are a field of study that deals with the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases and conditions of the oral cavity.

LAW

LLB | LLM

Law is a system of rules created and enforced through social or governmental institutions to regulate behavior, with its precise definition a matter of longstanding debate.

ARCHITECTURE

B.ARCH | B.PLANNING | M.ARCH | M.PLANNING

The art and technique of designing and building, as distinguished from the skills associated with construction. The practice of architecture is employed to fulfill both practical and expressive requirements.
